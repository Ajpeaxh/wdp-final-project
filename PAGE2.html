MECHANICAL KEYBOARDS





Mechanical Keyboards are not your typical keyboard. It has various parts that you can customize to you lifestyle or liking.

Mechanical keyboards are used by gamers and hobbyists alike. Compared to normal keybooards, mechanical keyboards have more

character.

You might have seen one of these around social medias. Various content of sound tests and reviews are
present on the internet.They are functional, aesthethic, and flexible. It can be modded to your liking.

If you are new to mechanical keyboards, this is your cheat sheet for it.

Mechanical Keyboards have 4 main parts which are important to know especially if you are new to them.

The Case, Circuit Board/Printed Circuit Board, Switches, and Keycaps.
