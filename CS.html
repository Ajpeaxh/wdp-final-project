KEYBOARD CASES





There are three main categories of Keyboard cases namely,


Metal Cases
Plastic Cases
and Wood Cases




Metal Cases

Common metals such as Aluminum and Stainless Steel are used for metal cases.
They proved a sturdy and edgy look for keyboards. Because it is made of metal,
it also adds weight into it. Depending on the thickness of the case, usually metal
cases compromise the sound but not it's durability.




Plastic Cases

Plastic Cases are made of materials which are easier to mass produce. Which then makes it
much cheaper than Metal Cases. Plastic cases are commonly used on prebuilt keyboards,
usually in black or white. However, other colors are also made available.
Plastic cases are much more lightweight and a tad bit less sturdier that metal cases.
Plastic cases tend to accumulate more oil and are much brittle and fragile than metal cases.
Nonetheless they offer a good case for keayboards, considering the weight of the keyboard
plastic cases are the go-to cases.




Wood Cases

Wood Cases, is without a doubt a heavy case. They are not that common than plastic and metal cases
but they still exist. Wodd cases offer an aesthetic look for the keyboard. Because of the thickness
of the case it affects the sound of the keyboard.
